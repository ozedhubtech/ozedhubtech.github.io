Reclaiming Data Sovereignty in a Decentralized World

Ozed Network is a Layer-1 blockchain infrastructure designed to give individuals and organizations verifiable ownership, control, and governance over their data.

In today’s digital economy, data is everywhere—but true ownership is nowhere. Ozed Network addresses this gap by embedding data sovereignty, transparent governance, and cross-chain interoperability directly at the protocol level.
